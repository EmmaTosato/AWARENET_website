Milestones

The network continues to grow by involving new partners and supporting community-led projects.

2019

AWARENET is founded with the goal of connecting Italian excellence in responsible research.

2021

Launch of the first joint labs with public entities and companies for interpretable AI pilot projects.

2023

Start of a mentoring program for early-stage startups developing ethical AI solutions.

Learning circles

Monthly sessions feature case studies from members who apply responsible AI practices in production.
